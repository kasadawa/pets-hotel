Our pets hotel is proud to be a top quality cattery and boarding kennels that always extends a warm welcome to both pets and their owners.


At our Pets Hotel our pet accommodation is designed to prove the best environment for your pet. We provide stress free, clean, dry and warm boarding rooms so that your pets can have their own holiday too! Every pet left in our care is ensured personal attention from our well trained and competent staff. We do not charge extra administering medication or special diets. We understand that all pets have their own personalities, traits and individual needs and treat them accordingly with love and attention. Cozy Pets Hotel really is a home from home run by responsible pet lovers. Caldecote Cozy Pets Hotel warmly welcomes visitors to view our pet accommodation. If you are looking to board your pet, we understand that you will get piece of mind from visiting any potential boarding arrangements before making a booking.
